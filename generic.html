my story

Gotta catch 'em all!

I'd like to think of my life journey as a Pokemon trainer. The different types of Pokemon I want to catch are like the skills that I want to acquire. At first, I only wanted to be good at one thing, so it was kind of like training my starter Pokemon and waiting for it to fully evolve with no other Pokemon. I was good at baseball. Like, really good. But I realized that I needed more. Because there isn't a single Pokemon type that beats all others. What I mean by that is baseball was only going to get me so far. Shortly, I left the baseball life behind me and went searching for other Pokemon.

I caught a level three "PC Assembly" and a level one "Graphic Design". [MORE TO COME]
